About Cash Cleaner Simulator

Cash Cleaner Simulator welcomes you to the most satisfying job you never knew you wanted! It\'s part oddly satisfying cleaning sim, part fever dream. Every single banknote is physics-simulated, making the act of cleaning, sorting, and stacking cash an immersive experience.

Take on jobs from shady clients, upgrade your tools (washing machines, dryers, scanners, and more), uncover secrets in your lab, and maybe even figure out how you got into this mess... and if you can ever leave. Explore the key features on our main page or dive into our Game Guide.

System Requirements (PC)

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 and up
  • Processor: i5 2.2GHz 8th Gen and up
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 1060 4GB
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 6 GB available space
  • Sound Card: Integrated

  • OS: Windows 10 and up
  • Processor: i7 8th gen ~3 Ghz / Ryzen 7 and up

  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 2070 8GB
